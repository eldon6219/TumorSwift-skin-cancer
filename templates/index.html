Skin Cancer:

Skin cancer is a common type of cancer that develops when skin cells undergo abnormal growth. Early detection is key to successful treatment.

These are some tips for preventing Skin Cancer:

1- Limit Sun Exposure: Wear protective clothing and use sunscreen
2- Self-Examine Your Skin: Regularly check your skin for any changes.
3- Avoid Harmful Sun Exposure: Steer clear of the sun during peak hours (10 AM to 4 PM).
4- Wear Sunglasses: Protect your eyes from UV rays.
